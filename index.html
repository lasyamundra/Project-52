Interesting Facts About Camel

  • They Can Go For Weeks Without Drinking Water.
  • They Store Fat In Parts Of Their Bodies Known As Humps.
  • Humps Act As Heat Regulators For Camels.

Interesting Facts About Panda

  • Giant pandas (often referred to as simply “pandas”) are black and white bears.

Interesting Facts Flying-Cicada

  • Cicadas are members of the Cicadoidea superfamily.
  • A cicada’s eye color can be blue, red or white.
  • Cicada species vary in size, between 0.75 of an inch and 2.25 inches.
